Travel Information

Venue

The Southeast Asian Workshop on Nuclear and Hadron Physics will be held at Universitas Gadjah Mada (UGM), Indonesia’s leading university, in Yogyakarta—a vibrant academic and cultural city. The venue offers modern facilities, and on-campus accommodations are available.

Accomodation

Participants can stay at the UGM University Club Hotel, conveniently located on campus. The hotel offers comfortable rooms, modern amenities, and easy access to the workshop venue.

For those seeking other options, Yogyakarta has a range of accommodations, from budget-friendly guesthouses to international hotels, all within a short distance from UGM. More details on booking and recommendations will be provided soon.

Visa

Most participants, especially from Southeast Asian countries, can enter Indonesia visa-free for short stays. Visitors from other regions may require a Visa on Arrival (VoA) or an e-Visa, depending on their nationality. The Visa on Arrival is available for many countries and can be obtained upon arrival at Yogyakarta International Airport (YIA).

For the latest visa requirements, we recommend checking with the Indonesian Embassy or Consulate in your country. More details and assistance will be provided closer to the event.

Access

Yogyakarta is easily accessible via Yogyakarta International Airport (YIA) and is known for its rich heritage, including Borobudur and Prambanan temples.
