Overview

We are pleased to announce the Southeast Asian Workshop on Nuclear and Hadron Physics, which will take place at Gadjah Mada University, Yogyakarta, Indonesia, on August 19-21, 2025.

This workshop aims to bring together researchers from across the region to discuss a broad range of topics, including:

  • Nuclear structure and reactions
  • Nuclear astrophysics
  • Heavy-ion collisions
  • Hadron spectroscopy and structure

We invite interested researchers, including students and early-career scientists, to participate and contribute to the discussions.
