Overview

We are pleased to announce the Southeast Asian Workshop on Nuclear and Hadron Physics, which will take place at Gadjah Mada University, Yogyakarta, Indonesia, on August 19-21, 2025.

This workshop aims to bring together researchers from across the region to discuss a broad range of topics, including:

  • Nuclear structure and reactions
  • Nuclear astrophysics
  • Heavy-ion collisions
  • Hadron spectroscopy and structure

We invite interested researchers, including students and early-career scientists, to participate and contribute to the discussions.

Organizing Committee

The organizing committee consists of dedicated researchers from various institutions especially in Southeast Asia.

  • Chalis Setyadi (Gadjah Mada University, Indonesia)
  • Ahmad Jafar Arifi (RIKEN, Japan)
  • Parada Hutauruk (University of Indonesia, Indonesia)
  • Norhasliza Yusof (University of Malaya, Malaysia)
  • Daris Samart (Khon Kaen University, Thailand)
  • Jingle Magallanes (Mindanao State University, Philippines)
  • Daiki Suenaga (Nagoya University, Japan)

We also welcome additional committee members from other Southeast Asian countries to join and contribute to the event.
